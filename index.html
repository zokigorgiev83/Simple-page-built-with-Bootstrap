Some interesting facts about beer

The ancient Babylonians were the first to brew. They took beer so seriously, if someone brewed a bad batch, his punishment was to be drowned in it.

In the Middle Ages clean water was often difficult to find, many people choose to drink beer instead because the alcohol made it safer than water.

Beer is the world’s most widely consumed alcoholic beverage and the third-most popular drink overall (after water and tea). And beer is fat free.

Norway first aircraft hijacking was resolved after the hijacker surrendered his weapon in exchange for more beer.

The world´s strongest beer is Brewmeister´s „Snake Venom“. While regular beer usually have about 5% ABV, this Scottish killer has a stomach-burning 67,5% ABV.
